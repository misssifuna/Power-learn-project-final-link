From Empty Tables to Loyal Customers

A Human-Centered Branding Strategy for a Local Restaurant

The Human Story

Meet Kevin, a college student with KSh 100. Every day he chooses a busy food vendor over a restaurant with better facilities. Why?

The Problem

A restaurant with parking, sanitation, seating, and running water struggles to attract customers while a nearby vendor remains crowded.

Research Findings

  • Students prioritize affordability.
  • Many assume the restaurant is expensive.
  • The restaurant is hidden from the road.
  • Digital visibility is limited.

Proposed Solutions

  • Student meal packages.
  • Digital menus.
  • Social media campaigns.
  • Loyalty rewards.
  • Improved signage.

Lessons Learned

The project showed that customer perception matters more than infrastructure alone. Good branding begins with understanding people.
